# Phase 10 — Executive Risk Center

Status: Implementation Complete

## Goal

Add a leadership-level governance panel that summarizes AI workflow risk, policy decisions, firewall activity, approval queue health, sensitive access attempts, policy violations, and executive readiness posture.

## Implemented

- Executive Risk Center panel
- Risk distribution summary
- Decision distribution summary
- Tool-call firewall summary
- Approval queue health
- Sensitive access attempts
- Policy violation count
- Human approval count
- Executive readiness posture card

## Executive Story

The lab now demonstrates operational workflow, governance evidence, and executive visibility in one interface.

## Boundary

This is a lab workflow demonstration. It does not provide production authorization, real regulated-system access, SOC 2 certification, real executive reporting assurance, or enforcement authority.
